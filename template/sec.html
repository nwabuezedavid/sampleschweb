SECONDARY

  • Secondary at LPSS.

    Our Secondary School caters for students from Year 7 to Year 11 (Ages 11+ to 16+) making up the two secondary Key Stages 3 and 4. The students follow the National Curriculum for England at Key Stage 3 (Years 7, 8 and 9) before selecting from a wide range of subjects to study for IGCSE examinations.
  • All students follow a common curriculum based on the National Curriculum for England. The following subjects are studied:
    • English
    • Mathematics
    • Science (Biology, Physics & Chemistry)
    • Modern Foreign Language (Italian, French)
    • Geography
    • History
    • Business Studies
    • Computing
    • Physical Education
    • Art
    • Music
    • Swimming
    • Home Economics
  • In addition to these subjects, students also follow a programme of Personal, Social and Health Education. Towards the end of Key Stage 3, a Careers Educational and Guidance programme leads into the selection of IGCSE courses for Key Stage 4 (Years 10 and 11). There are a list of core subjects that every child is required to take and additional subjects of which they select 5 subjects:
    Core Subject
    • English
    • Mathematics
    • Separate Sciences
    • Physical Education (non-examination)
    Additional Subjects
    • Business Studies
    • Geography
    • History
    • Accounting
    • Art and Design
    • Computing
    • Music
    • Economics
    • Global Perspectives
    • French
    • Literature In English

  • Our aim at LPSS is to enable our students, however long they are with us, to grow into kind, thoughtful and responsible citizens of the world. With this in mind, we provide all students, from Year 7 up to Year 11, with the highest levels of pastoral support, as well as academic challenge, that ensures that the school environment is nurturing and safe and therefore conducive to the highest standards of learning.
    Furthermore, we encourage students to develop in a much broader sense, through the many cultural, community, artistic, sporting and charity activities that feature prominently in our extra-curricular calendar.
    Our unique blend of curriculum programmes ensures that whilst retaining our ‘British’ connections to the UK educational system, we also offer world class internationally accredited examination programmes that enable our students to transfer between other British international schools if needed, as well as access an array of global tertiary opportunities.
