THE POWER OF THE Trinity NETWORK

Trinity College alumni are remarkable for their intellectual energy and engagement, and for making the world a better place in ways large and small. Our dedicated alumni, donors and friends are involved in so many ways in both College and student-led initiatives. Thank you for your generosity of your time, talents and experiences as volunteer leaders, mentors and guest speakers. Your donations support our distinctive educational programs, innovative student services, exceptional library and magnificent buildings. Your gifts create a legacy to benefit future generations of Trinity students. Your involvement inspires our students and enriches the Trinity experience.

Wherever your journey takes you, we hope you will stay connected to the College and your classmates. We look forward to welcoming you back to campus soon!
