Apply: Theology

Trinity College’s Faculty of Divinity is one of the oldest centres for theological study in Canada. Among the many advantages of studying at Trinity, our theological education and graduate degree programs are interdisciplinary and reflect contemporary liturgical, spiritual and pastoral practices. We are a vibrant and inclusive community, with students coming from numerous ecumenical backgrounds and from across Canada, the United States and around the globe. Our outstanding faculty specialize in biblical, historical, pastoral and theological studies. Our offering of theological studies with a concentration in Orthodox and Eastern Christian Studies, spanning both Eastern and Oriental Orthodox traditions, is unique in Canada.

Located on an inspiring, historic campus in a multicultural city, Trinity is affiliated with the University of {{site.country}}and the {{site.country}}School of Theology, which is ranked among the top ten training centres for Theology and Religious Studies in the world by the QS World Rankings. We offer financial support (up to 70% to 100% of tuition costs) to assist students in making time for their studies with us.

Whether you are interested in preparing for ministry, teaching, research, social justice work or other vocations, we invite you to start your journey with us. For more information, contact Trinity’s Faculty of Divinity at divinity@Trinity.utoronto.ca.
