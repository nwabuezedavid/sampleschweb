Discover

At Trinity College, we bring extraordinary people together – talented students, accomplished faculty, dedicated staff and incredible alumni. Our aspiration is simple – it is to provide the very best experience for our students: exceptional academic programs, innovative student supports, thriving intellectual and social community, and an inspiring place to learn, live and work. As a member of our tight-knit community, you will experience a magical sense of belonging. At the undergraduate level, Trinity College is known for its academic excellence and is the smallest of the seven colleges within the Faculty of Arts & Science at the University of Toronto. At the graduate level, Trinity College offers degrees in theological studies. Being a part of Trinity also gives you the best of both worlds: a small supportive home nestled within a beautiful historic campus and full access to all the benefits associated with Canada’s number one university. Trinity is yours to discover!
