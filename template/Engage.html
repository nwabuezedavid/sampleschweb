STUDENT LIFE & SERVICES

At Trinity College, you will find a vibrant and diverse student community. Whether you are a Trinity undergraduate or theology student, live off-campus or live in residence, or are starting first-year of university or graduating in June – there are endless ways to get involved with the Trinity community.

We want your experience – both in and outside of the classroom – to be the very best. Our dedicated staff and faculty work together to support the whole student and to ensure your academic success. You will have many opportunities to interact and network with our incredible faculty and fellows at college events and co-curricular activities. You will also have many opportunities to connect with our talented alumni for career advice, mentorship and other programs.

The College is known for our traditions, but it’s our sense of community that makes Trinity so special. Make the most of your Trinity experience – take advantage of all that the College has to offer!
