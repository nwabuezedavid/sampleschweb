Student Services

As a student at Trinity College and the University of Toronto, you have access to a number of excellent services offered by both institutions.

There are a number of offices at Trinity College that provide services to students, and these services all work closely together to support you! You can approach any one of them, knowing they can help you or connect you with other resources. Make sure to check out the services provided by each office (below) so that you know where you can get assistance for your particular needs. Please don’t hesitate to contact us if you have questions about any of these services. Further information is also available in our ‘Trinity Life’ module on Quercus!

The Welcome Desk

The Trinity College Welcome Desk is located in the front foyer of the Trinity College building at 6 Hoskin Avenue, and is open 24 hours a day. Staff at the Welcome Desk provide service to Trinity community members including answering general questions, renting lockers, helping residence students with parcel pick-ups, providing assistance when students are locked out of their rooms, placing work orders, signing out keys for bookable spaces around the College, and more. Stop by and meet our friendly Welcome Desk staff members!

Lockers: Lockers at Trinity are rented on a first-come, first-served basis at a fee of $30 for the year ($25 for renewals). Lockers must be rented at the Trinity College Welcome Desk with your TCard.

Office of the Registrar

The Office of the Registrar provides all Trinity students with academic, financial, and personal advising. The advisors in our office will listen, offer answers, options, clarification, and advice, and can also refer students to other more specialized services offered by Trinity and the University of Toronto.  Get in touch if you have questions, have encountered difficulties, or simply want to talk to someone about your plans.

The Registrar’s Office front desk is open for in-person drop-in questions – find our office hours below!  You can stop by the Student Services Centre if you have a quick question, want to book an appointment, or need a form filled out.  You are also welcome to send us a message at trinity.registrar@utoronto.ca – we can help out by email or set up an appointment for a one-on-one meeting with an academic advisor.  We are also regularly holding drop-in hours online – see details below on how to connect.

Office of the Dean of Students

The Office of the Dean of Students is responsible for oversight of the non-academic student life at the College, with the ultimate goal of fostering the well-being and academic success of Trinity students.

This entails several focus areas:

  • Working one-on-one with students to support them and advocate on their behalf
  • Working directly with student leadership to ensure student representation and delivery of appropriate supportive and social programming initiatives
  • Connecting students with appropriate resources within Trinity, the University of Toronto, and beyond
  • Oversight of residence admissions and placements; school-year management of residence operations
  • Emergency and crisis response and liaising with appropriate resources
  • Oversight of the College’s Health & Wellness Program
  • Supervision of the student life aspect of the Academic Don staff portfolio
  • Interpretation and application of relevant non-academic policies, also their development and revision through governance
  • Liaising with the University of Toronto to ensure optimal delivery of services

Kristen, Ramata, Cleofe, Cameron, Emily and Erin are available to speak with students for any and all matters. Please drop by our office or set up an appointment by emailing trinity.deanofstudents@utoronto.ca.
