  • Welcome to LPSS

    Early Years, Primary & Secondary

    Dancing Our Way Into The New Academic Year!

  • Early Years

    18 months - 5 years+
    The school adopts the Early Years Foundation Stage Curriculum (EYFS) from Creche to the end of their Reception year.
    Seesaw is a secure online Learning Journal for families which we use in the Early Years Foundation Stage to build up a record of children’s experiences during their time with us. This system allows us to work with parents to share information and record children’s play and learning inside and outside the classroom.

  • Primary

    5 years+-11 years+
    In Key Stages 1 and 2, our children engage with the English National Curriculum. At the end of Year 6, they take the (CAT 4) GL assessments. Other statutory assessments they do include the GL baseline and progress assessments as well as our whole-school teacher assessments.
    Children continue learning French which they commenced from Early Years alongside specialist subjects such as Music, Art, PE, Swimming and Home Economics.

  • Secondary

    11 years+-16 years+
    Our secondary school curriculum seeks to foster deep learning. Using objectives from the British National Curriculum and the Cambridge Curriculum our students are encouraged to pursue academic excellence while developing themselves in other non-academic areas of interest. At the end of KS3, they receive focused guidance as they choose their preferred subjects in preparation for the Cambridge IGCSE examinations at the end of Year 11.

    • Our Values
    At LPSS, we nuture our children from 18months through to 16 years+.

    We believe education should be broad and balanced. That is why in addition to the core academice offerings in Mathematics, English, Science and Humanities, we include Sports, Music, Art, Technology and several Languages withing our curriculum.

    The school day begins at 8am with a short break around 10am and a longer lunch break just after 12pm. School closes at 2:40pm with the option of an after school club till 3:45pm.

    We provide a wide range of after school clubs for all ages so everyone can develop their interests.
