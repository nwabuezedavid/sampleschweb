LANGUAGES

  • Languages at LPSS.

    Studying Modern Foreign Languages at LPSS is a rewarding and exciting experience! With the introduction of the Secondary school, LPSS has remained faithful to its vision and mission of creating an environment of international learning and has thus expanded its languages department to offer more Modern Foreign Languages.

    French has always been taught at LPSS and now Italian, Chinese, and Japanese have been introduced to the curriculum. We offer morning lesson for revision and a chance to explore the new languages for those who are not studying them yet. Our children are also prepared for international tests such as DELF for French and HSK for Mandarin Chinese.

    LPSS offers language and culture clubs (including the three major Nigerian languages and Spanish) and we organise several educational trips to explore the language and the culture in some of the countries where these languages are spoken.
